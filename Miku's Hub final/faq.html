Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Answers to common queries about Miku’s Hub services.

1. How long does it take to receive diamonds?

Typically 1–5 minutes after UTR confirmation. Occasionally, it may take up to 15 minutes.

2. What is UTR?

UTR is the Unique Transaction Reference number from your UPI payment, used to verify your order.

3. Can I make multiple purchases?

Yes, multiple orders are allowed. Ensure each order has a unique UTR.

4. What if I enter the wrong Game ID or Server ID?

Orders with incorrect details cannot be refunded. Always double-check your entries.

5. Who can I contact for support?

Reach out via WhatsApp with your order details.
